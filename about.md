I like reverse engineering old video games. I've messed around with the NDS pokemon games the most (the mainline games).

In particular, I'm looking at the inner workings of audio processing modules in the NDS handhelds: how the games structure audio information, and how it is processed by the hardware to emit sound! cool stuff 👍
